I write this book in memory of my late father Larry Rogers, who passed away in 2002 succumbing to the side effects of chemotheraphy treatment for a cancer that went undetected for many years.

This book is a collection, a collage, of the good memories, and sometimes not so good memories, of me and my father, mostly in work and mindset, but occassionally including personal stories as well.

I have allowed this book to be digitised and put on the internet since 03 March 2016.

- Norman Rogers.
